Privacy Policy for Find Nutrition

Effective date: 06/11/2025

We collect health and nutrition data you provide to personalise your experience. This includes your profile information, food logs, and health metrics. With your permission, we may access data from Apple Health to provide better recommendations. You control what data is shared through your device settings. Your data is used solely to provide nutrition recommendations and app functionality. We do not sell your personal health information to third parties. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and you use the app at your own risk. Your data is stored locally on your device and in secure cloud storage. You can delete your data at any time by deleting the app.
